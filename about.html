About True Light

True Light provides comprehensive lighting solutions backed by a team of specialists across different lighting segments, supporting clients throughout the full project cycle.

The company focuses on professional lighting design, concept development, value engineering and supply, with the capability to develop new products based on project requirements.

Group & Management

True Light Trading Co. L.L.C is part of a broader regional group with interests in banking, real estate, warehousing, education, car rentals and heavy equipment, in addition to lighting.

The enterprise is led by Managing Director NVSS Sarma, who brings extensive experience in the lighting industry, supported by a technical team for design and project execution.

Capabilities

  • Professional lighting design and documentation.
  • Value engineering and optimization of lighting schemes.
  • Supply of indoor, outdoor, architectural and specialty lighting.
  • Support for street, industrial, emergency and decorative lighting requirements.

Application Areas

  • Star hotels, restaurants and retail environments.
  • Office and commercial spaces.
  • Residential, landscape and facade lighting.
  • Street, industrial and specialized applications.
